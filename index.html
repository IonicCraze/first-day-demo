Rush Tribute: A Farewell To The Kings

Rush was a Canadian rock band formed in Toronto, Canada in 1968. In its early days, they went through a few member changes ultimatly arriving to their final
formation in 1974 with Alex Lifeson (guitars, composer), Geddy Lee (bass, vocals, keyboards, composer), and Neil Peart (drums, percussion, lyricist).

Throughout their career they have achieved numerous milestones, including: More than 40 million records sold worldwide, countless sold-out tours, a star on
the Hollywood Walk of Fame, inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, Officers of the Order of Canada.
